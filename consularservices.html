How to proceed?


Marriage Certificate


1.Request the “single status” document from the civil status register of your constituency in Equatorial Guinea.

2.Do not forget that the act of celibacy must be legalized both in the civil status register of your district and at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

3.Download and complete the certificate request document by checking the “single” option.

4.Attach to the file a color copy of your passport only the page on which your information is indicated.

5.Send your FULL file to the Embassy’s email equatorialguineaembassy@gmail.com.

6.The consular service will contact the applicant to offer a personal meeting and instructions to finalize the process.

5.The consular service will contact the applicant to notify him of the resolution of his file.


Terms


The Embassy of Equatorial Guinea to the Republic of Ghana grants the following types of visas: business, tourism, family visit, transit and religious.

Applications must be sent on Monday and Wednesday from 10:00 a.m. to 12:30 p.m., and visa pick-up will be done on Tuesday and Friday from 2:00 p.m. to 3:45 p.m.

The passport presented must be valid for at least 6 months, from the date of completion of the trip.

Your file must include:

      1.

      Two copies of the visa application document, duly completed and signed by the applicant. An unsigned and / or incomplete form will be rejected.

      2.

      Two recent passport-size photographs of the applicant in color.

      3.

      Certificate of good life and good morals sent by the police or the municipality (in English, French or Spanish), valid for 3 months after the date of issue.

      4.

      Return plane ticket.

      5.

      Vaccination file containing the vaccine against yellow fever.

      6.

      Letter of invitation legalized by the authorities of the emigration service of Equatorial Guinea. This letter must contain the applicant’s name and passport number and be valid for 3 months from the date of shipment.

The processing of visa applications generally takes two days. Applicants are responsible for depositing and collecting their passports at the Embassy.

Incomplete applications will be categorically rejected.

In case of refusal of your visa application, the file will remain at the embassy. Only the passport will be withdrawn.

The Embassy reserves the right to request additional documents from candidates (if necessary), as well as personal interviews.

The Embassy declines all responsibility in the event of possible loss of the passport.

Once the above documents have been compiled, applicants should submit the following additional documents, depending on the visa type: submit the following visa application:

      1.Business

          Invitation from the company receiving you in Equatorial Guinea, with a detailed explanation of the reasons for your trip.

      2.Tourism

          Letter from your bank confirming that you have a minimum of 2,000 euros in your account. Hotel reservation.

      3.Family visit

          Invitation of your family to Equatorial Guinea

      4.Religious

          Invitation of your contact in Equatorial Guinea. Authorization from the Ministry of Justice, Worship and Penitentiary Establishments (in Malabo or Bata).
