Our Services

The embassy of Equatorial Guinea in Accra may provide a range of consular services such as visa and passport processing as well as document legalization.

Visas

immigrants Visa applications in all visa classes are processed in accordance with document-qualified case and priority dates and Department of State scheduling guidance.

Consular card

Some governments issue consular cards to their citizens living in foreign countries. They can be used, for example, by an embassy to allow its citizens to vote in a foreign country.

Affidavit

An affidavit is a written sworn statement. When you use an affidavit, you affirm that the information contained in the document is true and correct to the best of your knowledge.

Passport renewal

A passport is an official travel document issued by a government that contains a person's identity A passport is used to verify the country of citizenship

Nationality Certificate

Important document used to prove where a person was born and where their family resided at a given time, nationality certificates can be obtained at embassies or consulates

Marriage and Celibacy Certificate

provides your current single status in case you want to get married abroad. The relevance of the document is to establish whether there are any obstructions that prevent you from getting married.
